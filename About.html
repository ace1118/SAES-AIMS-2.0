AIMS 2.0

Academic Information Management System v2.0

Project Title: SAES AIMS ( SAES- Academic Information Management System)


Problem Statement:
SAES has suffered a huge setback as a result of its sloppy data processing practices. This has a multiplying effect on subjects, results especially with the expanding population of students and employees. The school desired to implement a new state-of-the-art automated school management system , featuring a centralized data management system with constant back up, capable of supporting its major processes such as accessing instructional materials, modules, student and teacher record management, files, and reports in a fast-changing world where trivial procedures are rapidly phasing out.

Project Objective Statement:
To provide seamless accessibility of the necessary files of the teachers and to strengthen the paperless communication campaign; to adhere to the provisions of the Data Privacy Act of 2012, the Ease of Doing Business Law; to expedite procedures using ICT; and to develop a safe digital workplace.
The goal of this project is to create a website that is programmed with an effective record and identity management system that can be adapted to match the IMS and administrative records. Every administrative operation will be automated, and the school will be connected to the rest of the globe. Teachers will have access to the system, as well as students, who will be able to verify and print learning materials and files from their personal computers, tablets, or smart phones.

AIMS 2.0 is powered by Project LOGIn.

Project Title: Project LOGIn (Learning On the Go INitiative)

During module distributions, there are days that attendance showed a low rate of self-learning modules pick-upped by the parents/guardians of my learners due to some considerable reasons, e.g., transportation issues, parent's personal issues, time-conflict, etc. These issues cause delays and may result in pupils' poor academic performance. Observing the same problem from my co-teachers, Project LOGIN: Grade 3 Q1 (app prototype) was created and launched. Project LOGIN's first project is a set of android mobile applications of offline PDF of DepEd Region 4A-CALABARZON Pivot 4A self-learning modules from grade 1 to grade 6 (see attachment no.1).

Other innovations powered by Project LOGIN are the windows 10 and android mobile applications of SAES AIMS, a school-based information management system.
